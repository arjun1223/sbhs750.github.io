About the Program

Real skills.
Real results.

SBHS Robotics is a student-run program at South Brunswick High School where students take a robot from concept to competition — designing in CAD, building with VEX hardware, and programming autonomous and driver-controlled systems.

"VRC puts classroom STEM concepts to the test on the playing field — students develop lifelong skills in teamwork, critical thinking, and engineering design."

This season — Push Back — the 750 program fields six competitive teams. Team 750S has qualified for the VEX Robotics World Championship in St. Louis, Missouri, April 21–30, 2026.
